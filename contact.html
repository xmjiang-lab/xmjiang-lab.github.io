如何到访 / Visiting us

蒋老师办公室位于上海外国语大学松江校区 5 号楼 156 室。从上海市区可乘地铁 9 号线至松江大学城站，转校车或步行至上海外国语大学松江校区（约 15 分钟）。海外合作者来访前建议提前邮件联系，我们可协助安排住宿与签证文件。

Prof. Jiang's office is in Bldg. 5, Rm. 156, on the Songjiang campus of Shanghai International Studies University. From central Shanghai, take Metro Line 9 to Songjiang University Town station, then transfer to the campus shuttle (about 15 min). Visitors from abroad are encouraged to contact us in advance; we can help arrange accommodation and visa documents.
